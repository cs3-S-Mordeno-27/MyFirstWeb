Academic Endeavor

by Sasha Isabelle A. Mordeno

In the institution of higher education, we commence,

In search of knowledge in every spark,

Our mentor, books and talks

Academics, a remarkable departure.

Pens in hand and thoughts racing,

We steer this intellectual perplexity along the way, frequently over water.

We tackle and seek truth in our pursuit of it.

We are awakening in the field of education.
